Welcome to Cal Poly’s Office of Conference and Event Planning located on the campus of Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo.

A popular location for conferences and events of all types and sizes, the beautiful Cal Poly campus is just minutes away from pristine beaches, state parks, landmarks, premium wine regions, and a wide assortment of outdoor activities to choose from.

The campus is located in San Luis Obispo, California on the scenic Central Coast, halfway between San Francisco and Los Angeles.

Described by National Geographic as California’s Garden of Eden, the Central Coast is an area rich in history, unsurpassed scenic beauty and a region offering a diverse geography that includes world famous beaches and picturesque coastal mountains.
